About Me

Hi, I'm Ersin SARI, a computer engineer with a passion for cloud computing and DevOps. I have always been fascinated by technology and its ability to transform businesses and improve lives. Over the years, I have developed a strong interest in cloud computing and its potential to revolutionize the way we build and deploy applications.

My journey into the world of cloud and DevOps began during my studies, where I was exposed to various cloud platforms and services like Amazon Web Services (AWS). I was captivated by the flexibility, scalability, and cost-effectiveness that cloud computing offers, as well as the opportunity to work with cutting-edge technologies and architectures.

As I delved deeper into cloud computing, I discovered the importance of DevOps practices in achieving efficient and reliable software delivery. The idea of combining development and operations to create a seamless and automated software development lifecycle fascinated me. I started learning about tools like Docker, Kubernetes, Jenkins, and Ansible, and became proficient in building and managing scalable and resilient infrastructure.

Today, as a computer engineer specializing in cloud and DevOps, my goal is to leverage my skills and knowledge to drive innovation and help businesses leverage the power of the cloud. I enjoy working in collaborative environments, solving complex problems, and continuously learning and adapting to new technologies and industry best practices. By automating processes, optimizing deployments, and ensuring the reliability and security of systems, I aim to contribute to the success of organizations and make a positive impact in the field of cloud and DevOps.
